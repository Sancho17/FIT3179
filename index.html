Our world in data

Research and data to make progress against the world's largest problems. Our world in data is a research team based at the University of Oxford who created a worldwide database which has information about nearly all countries. Database can be accessed from here

Mortality rates around the world

This choropleth map shows the infant mortality rates for 1000 infants are born.San Marino was the lowest with only 6.7 and another surprising addition was Puerto Rico with just 7.3 Quite interesting as it is labelled as a developing nation

Life expectancy vs. GDP per capita

Vertical axis shows life expectancy and the horizontal shows real GDP per capita in USD. GDP is the market value of all goods and services a country produces within a certain amount of time. In this case it is data from 2020
